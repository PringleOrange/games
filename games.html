Astrospace
Astrospace is a 2D space game where your goal is to shoot enemies and survive as long as possible.

Astrospace has a kills and score system. If you go below 0 score (You lose 1 score each bullet you fire and gain 3 score for every kill you get) then you die. Furthermore, every fifty kills there is a boss you must take down. Use W, A and S to move and Q, E to fire bullets.
